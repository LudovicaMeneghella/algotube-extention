AlgoTube

This is a Youtube extension.

YouTube is like a tv/radio, but the one who decides is the Algorithm. Algotube shows the algorithm, modifyng the interface, not the mechanism itself. Enjoy your observation!

In order to have the proper results you should:
  • log out from your personal profile on Youtube
  • activate AdBlock or any other extension that blocks advertising
  • set the speed to the maximum allowed (x2)
